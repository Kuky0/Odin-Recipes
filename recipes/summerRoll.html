Summer Roll Recipe

Description

The recipe of a SummerRoll are very various but typical they are made off:

  • Rice Paper
  • Shrimp
  • Pork Belly
  • Bun
  • Veggies like:
    • Cucumber
    • Roman Lettuce
    • Mint
    • Basil

Steps

  1. Grab your rice paper and dip it in a tray filled with cool water
  2. Lay down your rice paper on a plate (preferably not paper plate or else it will stick)

    3. This is where you begin to fill your roll

  3. Fill it in the middle with your ingredients
  4. From there, you wrap it like a burrito
  5. Take the bottom side of the wrap and fold it on top of the ingredients
  6. Next take both sides and fold it in and make sure it is straight
  7. Now you should grab the whole bottom and roll tightly until there is no more left to roll

    8. The Rice paper should be sticky so it won't unroll
